Colors

The base logo is black or white for general display. But when it comes to make a new logo based on the downloadable template, there are no limits! It can be every color and outcome as long as it’s relevant to the chosen theme. Keep in mind that the colors on the logo should be linked to the chosen 4 colorscheme. Picking own color scheme

Don't

1

Use way too many colors

2

Pick more than 4 colors

3

SUse colors that are too close to each other and look alike

4

Stick to the neutrals. Just do not use bland colors

5

Don't lose sight of your emotional goal: Don't choose colors on the basis of what you love and what you don't

6

Ignore the theme

Do

1

Refer to a color wheel to identify complementary colors

2

Focus on monochromatic colors in the same hue.

3

Use Color Tools like: Adobe Color and https://coolors.co

4

Be Creative!

5

Go find out what color schemes fits with the chosen theme.
