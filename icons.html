Icons

Icons are always displayed in a circle to express the overall brand identity feeling.

The icons are always displayed in a circle. This to express the roundness of the logo. The content inside the circle will therefore always be the same color of the overal background. The fill color of icons will be white unless the background is black then it will be inverted

The icons for number has the same rules as the general icons. The content inside the circle will therefore always be the same color of the overal background. The fill color of icons will be white unless the background is black then it will be inverted. The font inside the numbers is VAG Rounded. This font can be found and downloaded on the ‘typography’ page on this website
