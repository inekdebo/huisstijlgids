The logo of Flüüfff is based on it’s core values. The ‘ü’ is the main visual presentation. It has a bold typographic form with an infinitely morphing face. Depending on which theme Flüüfff will build it’s convention around

It’s build to present the name ‘Flüüfff’ to make customers aware about the umlauts. It’s also the first visual presentation that pops up in people their minds when presented with the name ‘Flüüfff’.

Let there be variation!

Even with background

Best way to present the logo is fully rendered in Blender. This because of the natural shadows and highlights. Below you’ll find more information how to change and use the logo and background according to the chose theme.

Because the logo is mostly shown with background it’s important to spend enough attention to this. The logo with background will be used in different applications such as: banners, posters, folders,...

Tips:

1

When choosing a background, keep the chosen color scheme in mind. Pick color with enough contrast. The colors on the logo have to match with the background

2

Don’t make a too complicated background. Keep in mind ‘Simplicity is te ultimate sophistication.’ Keep it simple.

3

Don’t add more lightning, 3D objects or typographics in the bakcground.
