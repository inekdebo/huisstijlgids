Folder

The brochure is an important means of communication. For many people the brochure is the first introduction to Flüüfff. That is why it is important that the brochure is drawn up in accordance with the house style rules and radiates a clear message.

Design specifications

  • • Folder: 2 folds
  • • Open format: 295x210mm
  • • Closed format: 100x210 mm
  • • Paper type: wood-free satin-finished mc, 170 g / m².
  • • Color: Full color recto / verso
  • • Text frames: 79 mm wide
  • • Ongoing text: Montserrat, 9 pt, 15 pt interline. The current text is 1 mm from the secundary titles.
  • • Main title: VAG Rounded bold, 81 pt, 78 interline or you can choose own font that matches the theme.
  • • Primary titles: VAG Rounded bold, 20 pt, 15 pt interline and the text color depends on the color of the solid area
  • • Highlight text: important words can be highlighted. We place those words in Montserrat bold
  • • Icons : in a column structure each icon has a short explanation.
