Typography

Typography contributes to the visual identity of an organization. With typography you can put your organization in the spotlight visually and radiate the positive atmosphere. These fonts were carefully picked to support the visuel identity

VAG Rounded

VAG Rounded was designed by Gerry Barney and published by Linotype in 2000. VAG Rounded contains 8 styles.
We only use VAG Rounded Bold.

It is used in baseline of the logo and for big titels.

You need a license in orde to use it!

Get the font demo here:
https://www.fontpalace.com/font-details/VAG+Rounded+Bold

Monsterrat

Montserrat designed by Julieta Ulanovsky. Montserrat is a useful typeface that can be used it in any design project. We use: Montserrat Regular, Montserrat Italic and Montserrat Bold. It is free for personal use and commercial use.
